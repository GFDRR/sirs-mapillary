Click any green school to browse the Mapillary photos near it (ranked camera-facing-the-school first) and open the panoramic viewer. Or pan/zoom to a town to see the street-level coverage lines.

School points come from OpenStreetMap (amenity=school). Coverage = one or more Mapillary images within 250m of a school point. The table and the green/red dots show results from a per-country random sample of 800 schools (seed=42). The 100m column is the working distance for taxonomy eyeballing; the 250m column is the loose feasibility bound.

Data note: Guinea's raw % inflates because some source rows share a single Conakry centroid; prefer the Unique % column for Guinea.
